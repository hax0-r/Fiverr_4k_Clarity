Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is included with the Streaming Box?

    Your Streaming Box package includes the streaming device, a remote control, a power adapter, an HDMI cable, and a user manual. Everything you need to get started is provided.

  • Is the Streaming Box compatible with my TV?

    The Streaming Box works with any TV that has an HDMI port. For older TVs without HDMI, an adapter may be required (not included).

  • Do I need an internet connection to use the Streaming Box?

    Yes, the Streaming Box requires a stable internet connection to stream content. For the best experience, we recommend a connection speed of at least 10 Mbps.

  • Can I install third-party apps on the Streaming Box?

    Yes, the Streaming Box supports the installation of third-party apps. However, any issues related to these apps are outside the scope of our support and refund policy.

  • What should I do if I experience technical issues?

    If you encounter technical issues, please contact our customer support team at infos@4kclarityshop.stor with your order details and a description of the problem. Our team will assist you promptly.

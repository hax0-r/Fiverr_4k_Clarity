Privacy & Refund Policy

Refund Policy for Streaming Box

At 4K Clarity, we understand that occasionally issues may arise, and we strive to ensure a fair and transparent refund process for our “Streaming Box” product. Here’s what you need to know:

Refund Eligibility Criteria:

  • Technical Issues: If you encounter technical issues with your Streaming Box that our support team cannot resolve within a reasonable timeframe, you may be eligible for a refund.
  • Product Discrepancy: In the event that the Streaming Box provided does not meet the specifications as described on our website, you may request a refund.

Terms of Service:

Refunds will not be issued if you have violated our terms of service. These violations may include but are not limited to:

  • Unauthorized account access: Any attempt to gain unauthorized access to our services or another user’s account is considered a violation of our terms of service.
  • Sharing account credentials: Sharing your account login details with others or allowing multiple users to access our services through a single account is prohibited.
  • Commercial use restrictions: Our Streaming Box is intended for personal use only. Any attempt to use it for commercial purposes without prior authorization is a violation of our terms.
  • Content misuse: Any misuse of the content provided through our Streaming Box, including but not limited to copyright infringement, is strictly prohibited.
  • Fraudulent activity: Engaging in any form of fraudulent activity, including but not limited to payment fraud or manipulating our systems, is considered a violation of our terms of service.
  • Breach of security: Any attempt to breach the security of our systems, networks, or infrastructure is prohibited.
  • Abuse of support services: Misusing our customer support services, including making false claims or providing misleading information, is considered a violation of our terms

Please note:

  • Refunds will not be issued immediately upon detection of a violation. Instead, there will be a time delay of 24 hours to allow for a review of the violation and an opportunity for the user to rectify the issue if possible.
  • Refunds will not be available if more than 24 hours have passed since the initial use of our services or the Streaming Box, regardless of any detected violations.
  • If the violation persists after the 24-hour period, refunds will not be issued, and appropriate action may be taken, including account suspension or termination.

Third-Party Application Limitations:

Please note that while the Streaming Box may support various third-party applications, devices, or platforms, any issues, malfunctions, or dissatisfaction related to these third-party applications fall outside the scope of our refund policy. We encourage you to reach out to the respective third-party support teams for assistance with such issues.

Refund Process:

  • Initiating a Refund Request: To request a refund, please contact our customer support team at infos@4kclarityshop.store within the eligible refund period.
  • Providing Information: When initiating a refund request, please provide detailed information about the issue you are experiencing, including your order number and any relevant screenshots or error messages.
  • Investigation and Resolution: Our support team will investigate the issue and attempt to resolve it in a timely manner. If a resolution cannot be reached within a reasonable timeframe, a refund may be issued

Refund Decisions:

  • Case-by-Case Assessment: Refund requests will be assessed on a case-by-case basis.
  • Factors Considered: Factors such as the nature of the issue, its impact on the product’s functionality, and adherence to our terms of service will be considered in the refund decision.
  • Refund Method: Refunds will be processed using the same payment method used for the original purchase.

Exclusions:

Please be aware that refunds may not be provided if the issue is due to factors beyond our control, such as internet service interruptions or issues related to third-party applications.

Contact Us:

If you have any questions or concerns regarding our refund policy or wish to initiate a refund request, please don’t hesitate to contact our customer support team at infos@4kclarityprime.com

We are committed to ensuring your satisfaction and will work diligently to address any issues you may encounter.

Thank you for choosing the Streaming Box from 4K Clarity.
